The Exploring Consciousness workshop is a deeply immersive 5-day residential journey into self-awareness and inner transformation, designed to help participants explore multiple dimensions of consciousness. It bridges the gap between knowing and living through guided practices and experiential learning. Participants gain clarity, alignment, and a deeper connection with their inner self and life purpose.





Exploring Consciousness

Since its inception in early 2024, this workshop has followed a journey of its own. What began as a 3-day process, evolved into a 4-day journey in 2025, and will now unfold as a deeply immersive 5-day residential experience from 2026.

Guided by the Universe, the workshop has organically shape-shifted into an all-pervasive, expansive, and hands-on transformational journey.

Along the way, certain topics have naturally fallen off the radar, while others have metamorphosed into powerful, thought-provoking exercises and explorations - continually evolving in resonance with the times we live in.

This 5-day residential workshop has been designed to help each participant gain insights into exploring divinity within & bridge the gap between knowing and doing. We will be approaching this from various dimensions and views of 'Consciousness'.

As Dr. David Hawkins says, "All sentient beings are equal & all things are intrinsically holy in the divinity of their creation".

Exploring Consciousness workshop highlights:
- We will be working primarily against the backdrop of the 'Levels of Consciousness' by Dr. David Hawkins.
- Identify our soul's level of consciousness & what we can do to continue enhancing our soul's vibrational value.
- Ways in which we can feed our soul and make our life a gift!
- Understand what an 'attractor field' and 'field of dominance' are, and what actions can enhance/ deplete these in day-to-day life.
- Gain insights into the role of an 'observer', 'teacher' & 'spiritual guide'.
- Learn the art of 'behavioural kinesiology' ( muscle testing for hands-on exercises).
- Explore views of quantum physics and its obvious relationship to consciousness.
- Explore the tool of pendulum dowsing & its applications in the space of consciousness
- Delve into the work of Ramana Maharshi, texts from the Rig Ved, Carl Jung, Abraham Hicks, Dr. Rupert Sheldrake, Dr. Iain McGilchrist among many others to understand the various views on the concept of consciousness.

We have an opportunity to work hands-on with our physical bodies, the projects we are engaged with and aim to undertake, our relationships, the books we read, the people we choose to be surrounded by etc. We can make measurable changes in every aspect of our life that is of importance and value to us with this new knowledge.
