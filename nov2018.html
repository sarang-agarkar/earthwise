Hello dear telepathic communicators,

The last 5 years have been heartwarming especially with the community of animal communicators growing in India. For those who have learnt & honed your telepathic abilities to communicate with the animal kingdom, here is an opportunity to delve deeper into the science and further expand your horizons.
