The greatest gift you can give an animal is to truly listen—not with your ears, but with your heart.

Basic Interspecies Communication is a gentle and empowering practice that helps you connect with animals beyond words. It invites you to tune into their emotions, thoughts, and perspectives, creating a deeper bond rooted in trust, awareness, and mutual understanding.





Online Basic Interspecies Communication Workshop

This ancient art of interspecies telepathic communication is insightful, empowering and humbling in equal measure. Whether it’s your pet or a rescue animal you are working with, every human is capable of dialogue with these sentient beings.

Workshop Highlights:
- Have you wondered what your pet is doing at home while you are away at work?
- Would you like to know your pet's food preferences?
- Learn what your dog/cat feels about their preferred activities
- Listen to the guidance your pet has for you on your health or your spiritual growth.
- Know an animal's view on ' the concept of being rescued'
- Have a word with lost animals to know if they are truly lost or if they have left by choice.
- Gain an insight on the concepts of Karma and Soul contracts
- Manage the layers within health and well-being & explore the varied healing modalities, for both animals & humans.

Learning Methodology:
- Each batch is an online group of 22 communicators.
- It is a total of 12 hours split into 6 sessions over 3 days.
- At the end of each session, the communicators will be paired up with other communicators for practice.
