About the facilitator:
Manjiri is a certified telepathic animal communicator having learned with Dr. Maia Kincaid, author, and founder of the ‘Sedona International School of Animal and Nature Communication’, Carol Gurney of the ‘Gurney Institute of Animal Communication’ and Kim Pickett, who is a ‘Bioenergetic Healer’. Manjiri has had the opportunities of working with both domesticated and wild animals, ranging from tigers, leopards, elephants, and sloth bears to cats, dogs, horses, birds, and trees. She also a certified Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) Master Trainer with NFNLP-USA and with this understanding of both human and animal behaviour, her role is that of a ‘medium’ between the person and their pet and hopes to increase compassion, awareness and mutual understanding.
