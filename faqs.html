Telepathic Interspecies communication
1. Does Manjiri Latey conduct all the workshops herself?
Yes, Manjiri conducts all workshops herself
2. What is the duration of the ‘Online Basic Telepathic Interspecies Communication workshop’?
The Online Basic Telepathic Interspecies Communication workshop is for a total of 12 hours split into 6 sessions over 3 days; usually a Friday-Saturday-Sunday. Since 2020, we have moved all our ‘Basic Telepathic Interspecies Communication workshops’ onto the online platform.

Yes! We have designed an ‘Advanced Telepathic Interspecies Communication workshop’ as the next step to having completed the basic workshop. The advanced workshop is a residential process spanning 4 nights and 5 days, usually conducted in a nature reserve in central India.

Telepathic communication is an innate ability we are all born with. Thus, anyone with an open mind & a penchant for learning can enroll for the workshop. Even those skeptical of this process are welcome to join & explore this ability and its applications.
5. What is the main language of instruction during the workshops?
The primary language of instruction is English, since we have people attending from various parts of the world. However, based on the profile of the participants, Manjiri does intermittently facilitate the content in Hindi/ Marathi as required.

NEURO LINGUISTIC PROGRAMMING (NLP)
1. What is Neuro Linguistic Programming?
Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) as the name signifies, encompasses the main pillars which are instrumental in creating a human experience; Neurology, Language and Programming. Our neurological structure controls how our bodies function, our language governs how we interact and communicate with people around us, and the programming defines how we create models (perceptions) of the world. NLP, is thus a science with which one can make their personal and professional lives more smooth sailing and meaningful
2. What is the duration of NLP workshops?
The NLP workshops are for a duration of 5 days, irrespective of the level of the workshop.
3. Are there higher levels to the NLP workshops?
There are 3 levels in the NLP workshops
A) The Basic practitioner’s workshop: which is a total of 5 days split over 2 weekends
B) The Master practitioner’s workshop: which is a total of 5 days split over 2 weekends
C) The Train the Trainers workshop: which is a total of 5 consecutive days
4. What is the reason that the 5-day workshop is split over 2 weekends?
NLP as a science is about bringing about real and measurable changes in everyday life. Having said this, the real test of a tool is in its application. Thus, after completing the first 3 days of the workshop, participants get an opportunity to go back into real life situations to apply the tools and techniques. They then return for the final 2 days of the workshop, the subsequent weekend, to go deeper into the understanding and applications.
5. What certification will I receive on completion of the NLP workshops.
Yes. After conducting these workshops under an American certifying body for the last 20 years, we are now choosing to curate and deliver NLP workshops under the umbrella of Earthwise. The aim is to design content that is true to our cultural roots and celebrates the work of the founders of NLP, along with numerous other scientists and researchers in this field of human excellence. The intention is also to make NLP accessible to more people, so that they are empowered to change their own lives.
6. How many participants do you have in each workshop?
Manjiri is particular about individual attention for every participant and thus the NLP batches have a maximum of 20 participants each; while for the other residential workshops like ‘Advanced Telepathic Interspecies Communication’, ‘SoulCraft’ & ‘Exploring Consciousness’ the group size is capped at 24 participants.
